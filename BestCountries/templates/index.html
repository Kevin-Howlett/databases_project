DB Project Best Countries

Welcome!

This is our interactive web app designed to investigate the question: "What is the best country in which to live?"

The below sections strives to assist users in answering this question for themselves by giving them a easy way to explore data collected on various countries provided by the sources cited at the bottom of this page.

Best Countries by Attributes

The charts below compare select indicators for education, health, workforce utilization, happiness, and military spending for each country (see next section for variable descriptions).

These indicators can be used to compare countries on the desirability for data scientist residence. Traditionally, for a viable data science career, a country needed to provide the proper business environment, technical infrastructure, and properly trained workforce. In addition, health factors are important for any resident of the country. Military spending was included because it is representative of the overall influence of countries, and when choosing the desirability of a country for residence, some individuals may prefer to avoid countries that overly invest in military powers.

An additional consideration is in the post-pandemic world, companies will be more open to employees and consultants working from the location of their choice. In this case, the major factors to allow a viable data science practice at the very basic level may only include reliable electricity and high-speed Internet service.


Navigating the plots:

The bar chart has each attribute min-max scaled (from 0 to 1). The main purpose for the scaling is to allow direct comparisons between the countries and various attributes in a single visual.
Hover over each bar for the country name associated with that bar, and other information. You may isolate any attributes you wish using the selector to the right by double clicking it. Double click again to revert to all attributes selected. Click once to select or deselect multiple attributes.

The box-scatter plots contain points for each country, over which you can hover your mouse to view the country name and associated value. Use the dropdown to the left of the plot to view single attribute description and bar-scatter plot.

Data Science Salaries by Country

We have included a chart of data scientist incomes below to help answer the only question all of us here are interested in:

"What is the best country for a data scientist to live in?"

This data was collected from the 2018 Kaggle ML & DS Survey, and include average pay by country for people with job titles:
"Data Scientist", "Data Analyst", or "Data Engineer".

Mouse over the bars to view country names and average yearly salary estimates.

Data Sources

The data used in this app was pulled from the below sources.


2018 World bank World Development Indicators

World Development Indicators (WDI) is the World Bank’s premier compilation of cross-country comparable data on development.


2018 Kaggle ML & DS Survey

The most comprehensive dataset available on the state of ML and data science


World Happiness Report 2019

The World Happiness Report is a landmark survey of the state of global happiness that ranks 156 countries by how happy their citizens perceive themselves to be. This year’s World Happiness Report focuses on happiness and the community: how happiness has evolved over the past dozen years, with a focus on the technologies, social norms, conflicts and government policies that have driven those changes.
