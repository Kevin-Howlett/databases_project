Best Countries by Attributes

This is an interactive web app designed to investigate the question: "What is the best country in which to live?"
The charts below contain data on various attributes that may help in answering that question (e.g. Life expectancy, per capita GDP, etc.).
The bar chart has each attribute min-max scaled (from 0 to 1). You may isolate any attributes you wish using the selector to thr right.
The box-scatter plots contain points for each country, over which you can hover your mouse to view the country and associated value.

Data Sources

The data used in this app was pulled from the below sources.


2018 World bank World Development Indicators

World Development Indicators (WDI) is the World Bank’s premier compilation of cross-country comparable data on development.


2018 Kaggle ML & DS Survey

The most comprehensive dataset available on the state of ML and data science


World Happiness Report 2019

The World Happiness Report is a landmark survey of the state of global happiness that ranks 156 countries by how happy their citizens perceive themselves to be. This year’s World Happiness Report focuses on happiness and the community: how happiness has evolved over the past dozen years, with a focus on the technologies, social norms, conflicts and government policies that have driven those changes.
